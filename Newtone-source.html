(01) — About

A small studio with a long memory .

Note from
the founders

Newtone is a three-designer studio in Navi Mumbai with nine years of combined work behind us — most of it spent embedded with global cybersecurity teams who need more than a freelancer and less than a 60-person agency.

We picked up the cadence of in-house teams: turn things around in a day or two, keep visual systems consistent across hundreds of deliverables, and stay reachable.

The work spans social to 3D, brochure to UI — but it all shares a posture: direct, considered, hard to ignore.

We're hiring, slowly, and only when the work calls for it.

(04) — How we work

Four steps, repeated weekly.

01

Brief

30-min kickoff. We map the deliverables, channels and dates onto a shared sprint.

02

Draft

First-pass concepts within 24–48 hours. Real layouts, real copy slots, no lorem.

03

Refine

Two structured rounds. We track changes in your tool of choice — Figma, Slides or Slack.

04

Ship

Source files, exports, and any motion variants. Archived and named the way your team likes.
